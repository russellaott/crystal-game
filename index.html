Crystal Collector Number Game

Rules to the Game


  1. 1. The computer generates a random number that is your winning number
  2. 2. Your goal is to click on the crystals to try to match the winning number
  3. 3. A random number is generated and added to your total each time a crystal is clicked
  4. 4. If your total score is more than the winning number you lose
  5. 5. If you match the winning number exactly you win!
